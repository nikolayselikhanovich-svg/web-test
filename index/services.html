Our Specialties

Full-Service Contracting

BNL Builders handles a wide range of residential and light commercial contracting work throughout the Newark, CA area and beyond.

Door Repair & Installation

Proper door installation is about more than aesthetics — it's about security, energy efficiency, and function. We handle full door replacements, new installations, and repairs for interior and exterior doors on residential and commercial properties. From frame damage to hardware replacement to weather sealing, we ensure a solid, lasting fit every time.

Interior & exterior doors Frame repair & replacement Door hardware & locksets Weather stripping & sealing Sliding & French doors Storm doors

Water Leak Repair

Water damage is destructive and spreads fast. Our team locates the source of leaks — whether it's a roof, wall, window, or plumbing penetration — and repairs both the cause and the damage it left behind. We work thoroughly so the problem is solved permanently, not patched temporarily.

Roof leak detection & repair Window & door leak sealing Wall & ceiling water damage Subfloor water damage repair Moisture barrier installation Mold remediation referrals

Drywall Repair & Installation

From a nail hole to a collapsed wall, we handle all levels of drywall work. Our finishers texture-match and blend seamlessly so repairs are invisible — not just functional. New construction, renovations, or restoration after water damage, we bring smooth walls back to life.

Patch & hole repair Drywall hanging & taping Texture matching Water damage restoration Ceiling repair Skim coat & finish

Interior & Exterior Painting

We prep right and finish clean. Our painting services cover full-room interior work, exterior repaints, cabinets, trim, ceilings, and more. We use quality paints from trusted brands and protect your floors, furniture, and fixtures throughout the job.

Interior rooms & ceilings Exterior siding & trim Cabinet painting & refinishing Garage floors & concrete Primer & surface prep Color consultation

Home Repairs

Something's broken and you need it fixed by someone who knows what they're doing. We handle a wide variety of residential repairs — from structural issues to finish work — with the same level of care regardless of the job size. No job is too small to be done right.

Structural repairs Floor & subfloor repair Window repair & caulking Trim & baseboard work Deck & porch repairs Fence & gate repair

Remodeling

Turn the space you have into the space you want. We manage residential remodeling projects from initial planning through final cleanup. Kitchens, bathrooms, garages, basements — we help you design a clear scope, stay on budget, and deliver a finished product you love.

Kitchen remodeling Bathroom remodeling Room additions Garage conversions Basement finishing ADU & in-law units

Framing & Finish Work

Solid framing is the backbone of any structure. We handle rough and finish framing for new construction, additions, and interior renovations. From wall framing to roof structure to detailed millwork, we build the bones and perfect the finish that makes a space look and feel complete.

Rough framing Wall & partition framing Roof framing Finish carpentry Crown & base molding Custom built-ins
