Who We Are

BNL Builders, Inc.

BNL Builders, Inc. is a full-service general contracting company based in Newark, California. We work with homeowners and property owners throughout the Bay Area to get projects done right — on time, on budget, and to a standard we're proud to stand behind.

We've built our reputation job by job, neighbor by neighbor. Our work speaks for itself — and so does our 5-star rating. Whether it's a small repair, a fresh coat of paint, or a complete home remodel, we bring the same level of care and attention to every project.

We're not a faceless contractor mill. We're a local company that answers its phone, shows up on time, and treats your home the way we'd want our own treated.
